One size does not fit all when it comes to update strategies. Validations, error reporting, security, and the like can change. When they do, if your single update policy is tightly coupled to a schema, it’ll hurt. The changeset lets Ecto decouple update policy from the schema, and that’s a good thing because you can handle each update policy in its own separate changeset function. {Programming Phoenix 1.4}
